Privacy Policy – Image Downloader: Right Click Save as JPG PNG
Last modified: May 11, 2026

Introduction
Image Downloader: Right Click Save as JPG PNG ("the Extension") is a Google Chrome browser extension which enables users to save/download images from websites as JPG/PNG files using right-click context menus.
It's our mission that we do not store any of your data at all. The privacy of our users is paramount, and hence we do not collect, track, process, store, or transfer any personal data.

What Data We DON'T Collect
The following data we DO NOT collect:

Personal details like names and contact email addresses
Website browsing history
URLs of images downloaded and images themselves
IP addresses
Device IDs
Usage statistics
Cookies/Tracking IDs

Third-Party Services
This Extension does not use any third-party service, analytics tool, ad network, or tracker.
No data will be disclosed to any third party, ever.

Children's Privacy
The Extension does not intentionally gather any information from users, including minors aged 13 years or younger. There being no data collected, there is also no need for privacy protection or disclosure.

Privacy Policy Updates
In case of any changes to this privacy policy, the "Last updated" date on this page will reflect those changes. Using the extension post-updates implies your acceptance of the new policy.

This Extension was made by an individual developer and has no affiliation with any company or organization, including Google, Chrome, and image hosting platforms.
